About This Server

We are a semi-serious Star Wars RP server, but we are much lighter than most other Star Wars RP servers out there!
Our Star Wars servers takes place during the Old Republic era, 4,000 years before A New Hope.
This one in particular is set on Nar Shaddaa, the Smuggler's Moon.
Nar Shaddaa is a moon rampant with crime whose rulers will do nothing to stop it.

Once again, welcome to our server!
Please read the rules in the MOTD before beginning to make the server more enjoyable for everyone.
Thank you.
